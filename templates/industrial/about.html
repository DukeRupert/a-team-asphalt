About A-Team Asphalt

Our Story

Built from the ground up.

A-Team Asphalt started in 1995. Not with a marketing plan — with a truck, a crew, and a reputation for showing up and doing it right.

Don and Gary are the foundation of this company. Second-generation tradesmen who grew up in the trade and have spent three decades learning exactly where other contractors cut corners. They built A-Team on the opposite premise: do it right the first time, stand behind the work, and give customers a straight answer even when it's not the one they were hoping for.

Thirty years later, the Puget Sound knows the name. That didn't happen by accident.
